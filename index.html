• Final Project Proposal for GEOG 464

For my final project I have chosen the ‘researcher’ project type. This final paper will try to determine if there is a correlation between socioeconomic status and prevalence and proximity to green space (parks, forests, etc…) in Montreal. I plan to incorporate the entirety of the island of Montreal as the study area and focus on visually representing the findings. This is an important topic as there is a well documented relationship between acessability to nature -and outdoor activites- and humam health and well being (both mental and physical). The results of this paper hope to highlight areas of Montreal that could benefit from investment in green space.

    This project will require at least the folllwing data:

  • Island of Montreal Census Tracts (Potentially DAs as well)
  • Land use data
  • Population and socioeconomic status indicators (median household income, education levels, family size)
